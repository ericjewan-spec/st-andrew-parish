Our Story

A Parish United
in Faith & Service

St. Andrew Parish is a vibrant fellowship of three Lutheran congregations serving communities across Guyana. Rooted in the rich tradition of the Evangelical Lutheran Church, our parish exists to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture the faithful, and extend God's love to every corner of our nation.

From the banks of the Demerara to the heart of Georgetown, our three churches — St. Andrew Lutheran, Swargia Vani Lutheran, and Sadhu Sundar Singh Lutheran — together form a diverse, multilingual community bound by grace, worship, and shared mission.

Our Churches

Three Congregations,
One Parish Family

Each of our churches brings its own unique spirit to our shared Lutheran faith, serving distinct communities across Guyana.

St. Andrew Lutheran Church

The anchor of our parish — St. Andrew Lutheran Church has served as a beacon of faith, fostering deep community bonds and spiritual growth through the proclamation of God's Word and Sacraments.

Chateau Margot, East Coast Demerara
Sunday Worship — 9:30 AM

Swargia Vani Lutheran Church

"Swargia Vani" — the Heavenly Voice. This congregation carries forward a heritage of worship that bridges cultures, proclaiming the Gospel with a heart for multicultural ministry and community upliftment.

Lusignan, East Coast Demerara
Sunday Worship — 7:00 AM

Sadhu Sundar Singh Lutheran Church

Named after the legendary Indian Christian missionary, this congregation embodies the same bold, selfless spirit — bringing the light of Christ to communities through passionate worship, teaching, and outreach.

Triumph, East Coast Demerara
Sunday Worship — 7:30 AM
